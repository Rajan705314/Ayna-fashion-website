WHO WE ARE

An Ultimate Manufacture Brand in Market with 20+ Experience

AYNA Fashion Industry is a fashion and lifestyle accessories manufacturing & export hub. With its headquarters in Ghaziabad, India the company holds years of experience in the industry and has carved a niche for itself over the years. An ISO 9001 certified enterprise, we have been privileged to work with the finest fashion houses globally. With current exports to multiple countries, we truly understand the pulse of the global consumer and trends set by our customers. Our offerings include Fashion Apparel, Jewellery, Bags, Scarves, Beach Wear, Footwear and Home Furnishing. Having been through the quality process of multiple brands, we are fully equipped in terms of infrastructure and supply chain to manage and execute assignments based out of India.
